About Us

We've experienced first hand the value in having a mentor who understands the challenges in breaking into a new career.

We're here to provide that same guidance.

We founded Employder, on the belief that even after spending an incredible sum at University both in time and money, that we were not fully prepared for our careers. We were well educated but lacked the skills, knowledge and most importantly the experience that would allow us to hit the ground running. We looked back and noticed that there was a very poor infrastructure to help students transition into their first career.

We noticed successful mentorship occurring within high-ranking executives, but the same support system was non-existent for those most in need of guidance. There were many resources available to students to help them, but often times the information was conflicting and the people providing it were far removed from the industry itself.

Employder's mission is to offer fully customized career development combined with an experienced industry mentor to provide you with the 'inside information' on what it takes to stand out to employers.

The job market today has never been more competitive; high grades and extracurriculars are no longer a guarantee to obtaining an interview or job offer. Our mentors have excelled in their careers and have recently gone through the same challenges you are facing. Our career development experts and mentors will ensure that you're equiped with a professional career image and the 'inside information' to land your desired job.
